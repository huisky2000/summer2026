# Germany Family Road Trip: DUS to STR

## Trip Context

- Family: 2 adults and 3 children, ages 10, 14, and 17
- Arrival: 8 July at Dusseldorf Airport (DUS)
- Departure to Barcelona: 11 July from Stuttgart Airport (STR)
- Night of 8 July: Best Western Wein- und Parkhotel Nierstein
- Nights of 9-11 July: Hotel Royal, Stuttgart city
- Goal: Drive from DUS to STR while enjoying interesting places, with a good balance of scenery, culture, and family-friendly stops

## Recommended Overall Route

Dusseldorf Airport -> Cologne -> Schloss Drachenburg -> Nierstein -> Heidelberg -> Technik Museum Sinsheim -> Stuttgart

Then use Stuttgart as the base for one full family day on 10 July.

## 8 July: DUS to Nierstein

### Suggested Route

DUS Airport -> Cologne Cathedral -> Schloss Drachenburg -> Nierstein

### Stop 1: Cologne Cathedral

Cologne Cathedral is a strong first stop because it is dramatic, famous, and easy to appreciate even if everyone is tired after the flight. It is also close to the route south from Dusseldorf.

Suggested visit time: 1-2 hours

Note: Tourist visits to the cathedral require a ticket from 1 July 2026. Tourist entry is generally possible Monday-Saturday from 10:00 to 17:45, with last admission at 17:30. Opening times can change because church services have priority.

Official site: https://www.koelner-dom.de/en

Nearby culture add-on:

- If the family wants a short museum stop instead of spending longer around the cathedral, use Cologne's central museum cluster. Cologne Tourism lists current exhibitions at Museum Ludwig, Wallraf-Richartz-Museum, Museum Schnutgen, the Museum of Applied Arts, and Rautenstrauch-Joest-Museum. Pick one only, because this is still an arrival day.
- Best fit for the children: TimeRide Cologne, a compact history/VR-style city experience near Alter Markt.

Cologne events calendar: https://www.cologne-tourism.com/experiences-lifestyle/events/calendar-of-events

### Stop 2: Schloss Drachenburg, Konigswinter

This is the preferred family stop after Cologne. It feels like a fantasy castle, has excellent Rhine views, and is more engaging for kids than adding another church or museum.

Suggested visit time: 1.5-2 hours

In July, Schloss Drachenburg is generally open daily from 11:00 to 18:00.

Official site: https://www.schloss-drachenburg.de/en/

### Optional Alternative: Koblenz / Deutsches Eck

If the flight arrives late or the family is tired, skip Schloss Drachenburg and stop in Koblenz instead for a short walk and dinner near Deutsches Eck, where the Rhine and Moselle meet.

### Evening: Nierstein

Check in at Best Western Wein- und Parkhotel Nierstein.

If there is still energy, take a short walk near the Rhine or around the Roter Hang vineyard area. The Roter Hang, or Red Slope, is known for red-colored vineyard hills above the Rhine and is a gentle way to end the first day.

Information: https://www.rlp-tourismus.com/en/experience/wine/stories/roter-hang-red-slope-prime-locations-in-rhine-hesse

Nearby culture add-on:

- Mainz is about 25-35 minutes from Nierstein by car. Mainz Tourismus describes "Mainz lives on its squares" as a July-August cultural program in the city squares, and the Rheinhessen calendar is the best place to check for wine, culture, and local events close to Nierstein.
- Practical choice: only do Mainz if you skipped a daytime stop or everyone still has energy. Otherwise, keep the Nierstein/Roter Hang walk as the cultural evening.

Mainz events: https://www.mainz-tourismus.com/en/explore-enjoy/events

Rheinhessen events: https://www.rheinhessen.de/en/what-when-where-1

### Recommendation for 8 July

Do:

- DUS -> Cologne Cathedral -> Schloss Drachenburg -> Nierstein

Skip:

- Koblenz, unless you arrive early and everyone still has energy

## 9 July: Nierstein to Stuttgart

### Suggested Route

Nierstein -> Heidelberg -> Technik Museum Sinsheim -> Stuttgart

This should be the main sightseeing day of the transfer from the Rhine area to Stuttgart.

### Stop 1: Heidelberg

Heidelberg is one of the best stops between Nierstein and Stuttgart. It offers a beautiful old town, river views, the Old Bridge, and Heidelberg Castle.

Recommended things to see:

- Heidelberg Old Town
- Old Bridge
- Heidelberg Castle
- Castle terrace views over the city
- Culture add-on: Kurpfalzisches Museum Heidelberg has a Regina Relang fashion photography exhibition running through 19 July 2026, and Heidelberg Castle Festival runs in the castle setting from June into early August. Check the daily program before deciding whether to add a performance.

Suggested visit time: 3-4 hours including lunch

Heidelberg Castle is generally open daily from 09:00 to 18:00, with last admission at 17:30.

Official site: https://www.schloss-heidelberg.de/en/visitor-information

Heidelberg events: https://www.heidelberg-marketing.de/en/events

### Stop 2: Technik Museum Sinsheim

This is the top recommended family stop for children aged 10, 14, and 17. It has aircraft, cars, military vehicles, engines, and large exhibits that are more interactive and memorable than a normal museum visit.

It is also conveniently located between Heidelberg and Stuttgart.

Suggested visit time: 2.5-4 hours

The Technik Museen Sinsheim Speyer are open daily, 365 days a year. In summer, opening is generally 09:00 to 18:00.

Official site: https://www.technik-museum.de/en/

### Alternative: Ludwigsburg Palace

Choose Ludwigsburg Palace instead of Technik Museum Sinsheim if the family prefers gardens, palaces, photos, and a calmer afternoon.

### Evening: Stuttgart

Drive to Stuttgart and check in at Hotel Royal.

If you arrive with energy left, take a short evening walk around Schlossplatz.

Nearby culture add-on:

- jazzopen Stuttgart runs from 1-12 July 2026. On 9 July, the program includes larger evening concerts around Schlossplatz and related jazz venues. This is a good add-on only if you arrive early enough and buy tickets in advance.

jazzopen: https://www.jazzopen.com

### Recommendation for 9 July

Do:

- Nierstein -> Heidelberg -> Technik Museum Sinsheim -> Stuttgart

This is probably the strongest family day of the route.

## 10 July: Full Day From Stuttgart

Because the family is based in Stuttgart, do not try to fit everything into one day. Choose one main plan.

### Option A: Tripsdrill Theme Park

Best choice if the children want a fun day.

Tripsdrill is under an hour from Stuttgart and has roller coasters, family rides, and a wildlife park. It works well for a family with children aged 10, 14, and 17.

In 2026, the theme park is open daily from 28 March to 1 November, subject to change. The wildlife park opens daily from 09:00.

Official site: https://tripsdrill.de/en

Culture nearby:

- If Tripsdrill is the main day, keep culture light in the evening: Stuttgart PRIDE Culture Weeks start on 10 July 2026 and run through 26 July at different city locations, with talks, screenings, exhibitions, readings, city tours, and cultural events.
- jazzopen Stuttgart is also still running on 10 July, with evening concerts in the city. This can turn the day into a long one, so treat it as an optional evening plan.

### Option B: Mercedes-Benz Museum and Porsche Museum

Best choice if the family likes cars, engineering, or design.

Both museums are in Stuttgart and can be combined in one day if you start reasonably early.

Mercedes-Benz Museum:

- Usually open Tuesday-Sunday, 09:00 to 18:00
- Closed Mondays
- Official site: https://www.mercedes-benz.com/en/art-and-culture/museum/visitor-information/

Porsche Museum:

- Usually open Tuesday-Sunday, 09:00 to 18:00
- Closed Mondays
- Official site: https://www.porsche.com/international/aboutporsche/porschemuseum/openinghoursandprices/

10 July 2026 is a Friday, so both museums should fit the schedule.

Culture add-on:

- This option is already culture-heavy in a design, engineering, and industrial-history way. If you want a more classic cultural layer, add Stuttgart PRIDE Culture Weeks or jazzopen in the evening.

Stuttgart PRIDE Culture Weeks: https://www.stuttgart.de/en/veranstaltungskalender/veranstaltungen/stuttgart-pride-kulturwochen-500835

jazzopen: https://www.jazzopen.com

### Option C: Black Forest High Road / B500

Best choice if the adults want scenery and the children are happy with a longer driving day.

Suggested route:

Stuttgart -> Baden-Baden -> Black Forest High Road B500 -> Mummelsee -> Freudenstadt -> Stuttgart

The classic scenic section of the Black Forest High Road runs about 60 km from Baden-Baden to Freudenstadt. It is known for forest scenery, viewpoints, and mountain-road driving.

Information: https://www.black-forest-travel.com/high-road/

### Recommendation for 10 July

Choose:

- Tripsdrill if the children want fun
- Mercedes-Benz Museum and Porsche Museum if the family likes cars
- Black Forest B500 if the priority is scenery

For this family, Tripsdrill is probably the safest and most enjoyable choice.

## 11 July: Stuttgart to STR Airport

Keep this day simple.

If the flight to Barcelona is early, go straight to Stuttgart Airport.

If the flight is later, possible light morning options are:

- Short walk around Schlossplatz
- Mercedes-Benz Museum, if not already visited
- Relaxed breakfast near Hotel Royal before driving to STR

Culture note:

- With the current plan to drop the car at STR at 09:00 and fly STR-BCN at 10:00, there is realistically no Stuttgart culture time this morning.
- After arrival in Barcelona and transfer to Sitges, use an easy Sitges cultural option: walk the old town and waterfront church area, or visit Museu de Maricel / Sitges Museums if the timing works.

Sitges cultural calendar: https://www.sitgesanytime.com/en/news/calendar

Barcelona City Council 2026 events: https://www.meet.barcelona/en/main-events

## Spain Stay Culture Ideas: 11-17 July

The Spain hotel for 14-17 July is not booked yet, so these are flexible options from the Sitges/Barcelona base rather than fixed transport plans.

### 11 July: Arrive Barcelona, Transfer to Sitges

Keep the arrival day easy.

Nearby culture:

- Sitges old town, Sant Bartomeu i Santa Tecla church viewpoint, and waterfront evening walk.
- Sitges Museums / Museu de Maricel if arrival energy is good.
- Barcelona alternatives on this date: Cruilla Festival is in its final day at Parc del Forum, while Grec Festival and Sala Montjuic are running in July. These are better only if the family actively wants a Barcelona evening after the flight.

### 12 July: Sitges / Barcelona

Nearby culture:

- Sitges Museums family treasure hunt, Museu de Maricel, Fundacio Stampfli contemporary art, or the Malvasia de Sitges Interpretation Centre.
- For a bigger city option, Barcelona Grec Festival continues through July with theatre, dance, music, and circus across the city.

### 13 July: Sitges / Barcelona

Nearby culture:

- Use this as a lighter local-culture day: Sitges old town, seafront, Maricel area, and one museum or exhibition.
- If going into Barcelona, look for Barcelona 2026 World Capital of Architecture activities. Barcelona City Council lists architecture programming from 12 February to 13 December 2026.

### 14 July: Spain Hotel Open

Nearby culture:

- If staying near Barcelona or Sitges, choose Grec Festival for an evening performance.
- If the family wants music in a landmark setting, check Bachcelona, Barcelona's Bach festival, which runs 7-16 July 2026.

### 15 July: Spain Hotel Open

Nearby culture:

- Sala Montjuic open-air cinema runs from 10 July to 5 August 2026 at Montjuic, with films, concerts, and picnic-style evenings.
- Alternative: Barcelona museums and architecture routes if the day is hot and you want more indoor time.

### 16 July: Spain Hotel Open

Nearby culture:

- Final practical chance for Bachcelona, which runs through 16 July 2026.
- Alternative: Grec Festival or Barcelona World Capital of Architecture activities.

### 17 July: BCN to DUS to TRD

Keep this as a travel day.

Nearby culture:

- Because VY1896 leaves BCN at 06:50, do not plan morning culture in Barcelona.
- The DUS layover from 09:05 to 13:55 is not long enough to comfortably leave the airport with a family and luggage. If the schedule changes dramatically, Dusseldorf Old Town and Rhine promenade are the closest simple cultural fallback, but this is not recommended for the current itinerary.

## Practical Notes

- Do not overload 8 July. Flight arrival days often take longer than expected because of luggage, car rental, food, and tired kids.
- Book or check tickets online where possible, especially for popular attractions.
- Keep snacks and water in the car. July can be warm, and the kids will enjoy the route more with fewer emergency food stops.
- For Stuttgart city, check parking near Hotel Royal in advance.
- For attractions, verify opening times again a few days before travel, especially if you plan around a specific timed visit.
