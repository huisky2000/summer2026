Booking And Airline Apps

Each card opens your saved app link first if you have one, otherwise the official website. Use "Set App Link" to save a direct link into the app, captured once from the app itself.

App deep links are not standardized across providers and change without notice, so each card lets you save your own link. Open the booking once inside the app, use its Share or Copy Link option, and paste the result into "Set App Link." Hotels.com is pre-confirmed to open the app directly.

    About This App

    All trip content lives in one file, data.js, so you can add days, fix bookings, or update app links without touching any other code. Open data.js in any text editor (including directly on GitHub: click the file, then the pencil icon) and follow the instructions at the top of the file.
